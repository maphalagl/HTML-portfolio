About Me

I am Lutendo George Maphala, a 22-year-old Black male, currently in my third year of pursuing a BSc in Information Technology at Richfield Graduate Institute of Technology. My passion lies in system development, and I am committed to honing my skills to make a significant impact in the IT industry.

From an early age, I demonstrated a natural curiosity and problem-solving aptitude, which drew me towards the world of Information Technology. After completing high school, I decided to enroll at Richfield Graduate Institute of Technology, a prestigious institution renowned for producing future IT leaders

In addition to my academic studies, I have taken the initiative to further enhance my web development skills by enrolling in a short course through Udemy. This online platform has provided me with the flexibility to learn at my own pace and explore various aspects of web development, including front-end and back-end technologies, web design, and user experience.

As I progress through my academic journey, my vision for the future becomes clearer. Upon completing my degree, I aspire to work in a dynamic and innovative IT company, where I can apply my knowledge of system development to solve real-world challenges. Furthermore, I dream of making a difference in my community by leveraging technology to create opportunities for underprivileged individuals

In conclusion, I am on an exciting journey in the realm of Information Technology and system development. With a commitment to learning and a strong drive to make a difference, I am confident that my unique perspective and skills will leave a lasting mark in the ever-evolving landscape of IT. The future awaits, and I am determined to embrace the challenges and opportunities it brings with open arms
